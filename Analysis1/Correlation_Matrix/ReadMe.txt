This is a 'correlation matrix' of the historical stock prices for the major public cannabis companies in Canada and the United States. The methodology used is pearsons correaltion on the adjusted daily closing prices combined with a heatmap for visualization. Legend will be green for a perfect correlation of 1.0 and red for perfect negative correlation of -1.0. This type of analysis is the foundation of a few hedge fund portfolio strategies, primarily the long short equity strategy.
